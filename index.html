Instructions For Calculating Payoff of Your Fixed Rate Mortgage

To calculate your mortgage payments, you need to know a few details about the loan. At a minimum, you need to know the loan balance, the interest rate, and the term of the loan in years. If you have the date of the first payment, you can include that; otherwise the current date is assumed. If you consistently pay above the minimum payment, enter that value as the actual monthly payment.

Press the Calculate button to display the minimum payment, total interest, interest saved, payoff date, and decrease to your loan term in the Calculations box.

The resulting Amortization Schedule will be displayed in the table to the right. You may enter any one-time increases to your payment or any changes to your payment going forward. Press the Calculate button after entering your data to recalculate your loan. These changes will also be reflected in the Calculations box.

At any time, you can toggle between the amortization schedule and a graph of your original and adjusted premium reduction by clicking the tabs under Amortization Schedule.
